Hierarchical Complexity

♠ Settlement hierarchy ♣ 2 ♥ levels. At the first level were semisedentary villages that the Iroquois lived in year round, but moved to new village sites every 10 to 25 years. The second level consisted of 1 to 4 villages that were part of tribal clusters, which could include chiefs in certain areas.[MAJIDBENAM_REF_5_UsIroqP]

Handheld weapons

♠ War clubs ♣ present ♥ There were war clubs used by the Iroquois, often made of wood or bone with "bone or stone inserted at the head."[MAJIDBENAM_REF_30_UsIroqP]
♠ Battle axes ♣ present ♥ The Iroquois used stone axes, until later in the 17th century when their war axes were made of European metals. Their axes were also used for woodworking, butchering, and hide scraping.[MAJIDBENAM_REF_31_UsIroqP]
