The Merovingian kings retained a double legal system, but with the growing anarchy of their rule, Latin memories and norms progressively faded. ♠ Name ♣ pilgrimage ♥ "Pilgrimage—journeying from one’s home to a hallowed site—has a long tradition. "Well over 1,000 centers of pilgrimage existed in France from the Gallo-Roman period to the end of the Middle Ages. [...]. "Pilgrimage was motivated by a variety of reasons.
